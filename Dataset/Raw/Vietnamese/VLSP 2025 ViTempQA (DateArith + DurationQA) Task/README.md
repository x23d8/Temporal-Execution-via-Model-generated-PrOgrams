## Terms and Conditions

The user of language resources developed by the VLSP consortium must respect the following understanding, terms and conditions:

1. The language resources may only be used for research and development of natural language processing, information retrieval or document understanding systems.

2. The language resources are not allowed to be used in commercial systems.

3. Do not redistribute these language resources. These language resources may be modified or improved to better serve a research purpose, but the edited language resources may not be distributed.

4. Summaries, analyses and interpretations of the linguistic properties of the language resources may be derived and published, provided it is not possible to reconstruct the information from these summaries.

5. Small excerpts of the language resources may be displayed to others or published in a scientific or technical context, solely for the purpose of describing the research and development and related issues.

6. Published research works which use these language resources must clearly acknowledge the VLSP consortium by citing its website or its corresponding publications.
